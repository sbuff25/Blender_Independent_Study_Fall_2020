###### Blender_Independent_Study_Fall_2020
 Repo for Blender projects
#### This repo will contain all files including detailed descirptions of workflows and strategies used over the course of the Fall 2020 semester as well as screen shots of models.
### This is an overview of project themes over the course of the semester
## * Weeks 1-5 (Aug 19th-Sep 19th)
    Low-poly modeling centering around the creation of low poly scenes that have an focus stylization vs realism.
## * Weeks 6-10 (Sep 20th-Oct 24th)
    This portion of the semester will be more rooted in sculpting and character modeling.
## * Weeks 11-15 (Oct 25th-Nov 25)
    This part of the semester aims to blend the previous weeks techniques and strategies. Weekly models will continue to be turned in but most of the focus will be on a final scene.
